School Projects

Neural Tangent Kernel: on Double Descent

This presentation discusses the double descent behavior of neural network training by linking it with that of kernel regression via the Neural Tangent Kernel. Along the way, the replica trick is introduced to derive the analytic formula for the generalization error of kernel regression.

Inverse Nonlinear Fourier Transform: Applications in Quantum Signal Processing

This report elucidates the application of nonlinear Fourier transform over SU(2) matrices on algorithm design for quantum signal processing. A compilation of algorithmic advancements such as the Riemann--Hilbert--Weiss algorithm are discussed.

Paper Review: Optimisation Geometry

Review of the paper "Optimisation Geometry" by Jonathan H. Manton.

Lindblad Master Equation: Generator of Markovian CPTP Maps

An overview on the Lindblad master equation.

Time-Frequency Methods and Optics: A Ray and Wave Analysis

This project uses the mathematics of time-frequency analysis to concretely state the relation between ray and wave optics.

Kendo Robot: Combining Robotics Technologies with Martial Art

A supervisory teleoperation kendo robot system. The robot is able to track the opponent’s pose and wait for the operator’s command to attack.

An Introduction to Quantum Computation in Geometric Algebra

Reformulating the mathematics of quantum computation in geometric (Clifford algebra).

Radiant Rhythm - Illumination on a Chaotic Pendulum

Ray tracing of a chaotic pendulum.

Stability Analysis on Replicator Games with Perturbations

This project analyzes the stability of games under the replicator dynamics. Special attention is given in dynamics where players take "surprises" into consideration, which acts as a perturbation.

Fourier Optics: Derivations and Applications

This project focusses on giving an intuitive but rigorous derivation of Fourier optics, including many of the complicated integrals related to Hankel transform, and we also dicussed a couple of its applications.
